# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

# 🎯 Project Overview

The E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales, revenue, profit, customer behavior, product performance, and regional business trends.

This dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, optimize product strategies, and improve profitability through data-driven decision-making.

# 🔍 Business Insights

## Revenue Analysis

- Generated ₹34.80 Lakhs in total revenue.
- Strong business performance across multiple regions.
- Revenue growth observed during peak sales periods.

## Regional Performance

- South Region generated the highest revenue (₹12.2 Lakhs).
- West and East regions delivered strong business contributions.
- North region has growth potential for future expansion.

## Product Analysis

- Bookshelf emerged as the highest revenue-generating product.
- Tables and Monitors showed strong customer demand.
- Product profitability varies across categories.

## Category Performance

### Electronics
- Highest revenue contributor.
- Highest quantity sold.
- Strong customer demand.

### Furniture
- Significant revenue generation.
- Consistent order volume.

### Office Supplies
- Additional revenue support category.
- Opportunity for business growth.

## Customer Segmentation

- Corporate customers generated the highest revenue contribution.
- Consumer segment maintained strong sales performance.
- Home Office customers contributed valuable business revenue.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

### Revenue Growth

- Increase marketing efforts in high-performing regions.
- Focus on expanding Electronics product offerings.

### Customer Strategy

- Strengthen relationships with Corporate customers.
- Create targeted campaigns for Consumer and Home Office segments.

### Product Optimization

- Promote top-performing products.
- Review underperforming categories for improvement opportunities.

### Regional Expansion

- Develop growth strategies for North Region.
- Analyze successful practices from South Region.
